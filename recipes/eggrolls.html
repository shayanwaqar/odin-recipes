Chicken Egroll


Description

These Southwest egg rolls aren't your traditional egg rolls. Small flour tortillas are stuffed with an exciting blend of Southwestern-style ingredients and deep-fried until golden brown.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • eggs
  • basil

Steps

  1. Whisk Eggs and melt butter
  2. Add eggs and stir
  3. Let it rest and add fillings
  4. Roll it up and garnish with seasonings
  5. Enjoy!
